02 — THE THESIS

🚀 Why Indie Wins by 2030

The game development industry is undergoing a structural shift more significant than the App Store moment of 2008. Three converging forces are dismantling the advantages historically held by large studios. By 2030, 50% of all commercially released games will be independently developed, up from 20% in 2025.

🤖

AI ELIMINATES THE PRODUCTION GAP

A solo developer with the right AI toolchain now ships what took a team of 8 two years ago. The marginal cost of production is collapsing.

💸

INFRASTRUCTURE COSTS ARE AT ZERO

What cost $500K in 2010 to stand up costs $0 in 2026 to prototype. The barrier is no longer technical — it is visibility and monetisation.

📣

COMMUNITY IS THE NEW MARKETING BUDGET

Telegram, Discord, TikTok and YouTube Shorts have made organic distribution achievable without agency spend. Players who earn rewards become advocates.
